Morning Prayer

Lauds • Thursday, Week 2

Opening Verse

God, come to my assistance.

— Lord, make haste to help me.

Glory to the Father, and to the Son, and to the Holy Spirit: as it was in the beginning, is now, and will be for ever. Amen. Alleluia.

Hymn

Lord of all hopefulness, Lord of all joy, Whose trust, ever childlike, no cares could destroy, Be there at our waking, and give us, we pray, Your bliss in our hearts, Lord, at the break of the day.

Psalmody

Antiphon 1

At daybreak, Lord, I offer my prayer to you.

Psalm 63:2-9

O God, you are my God, for you I long; for you my soul is thirsting. My body pines for you like a dry, weary land without water.

So I gaze on you in the sanctuary to see your strength and your glory. For your love is better than life, my lips will speak your praise.

So I will bless you all my life, in your name I will lift up my hands. My soul shall be filled as with a banquet, my mouth shall praise you with joy.

Short Reading

ROMANS 13:11-12

It is now the hour for you to wake from sleep, for our salvation is closer than when we first accepted the faith. The night is far spent; the day is at hand. Let us then cast off deeds of darkness and put on the armor of light.

Gospel Canticle

Antiphon

In the tender compassion of our God, the dawn from on high shall break upon us.

CANTICLE OF ZECHARIAH

Blessed be the Lord, the God of Israel; he has come to his people and set them free.

He has raised up for us a mighty savior, born of the house of his servant David.

Through his holy prophets he promised of old that he would save us from our enemies, from the hands of all who hate us.

Intercessions

Let us pray to God our Father, who sent the Holy Spirit to be the light of our hearts:

Lord, fill us with your light.

— We thank you for the gift of a new day, help us to live it for your glory.

May your wisdom guide our actions today.

— That we may seek first your kingdom in all things.

The Lord's Prayer

Our Father, who art in heaven,

hallowed be thy name;

thy kingdom come,

thy will be done

on earth as it is in heaven.

Give us this day our daily bread...

Concluding Prayer

Lord, as a new day dawns, send the radiance of your light into our hearts. Banish all darkness of sin and guide us safely into your eternal presence.

We ask this through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son, who lives and reigns with you and the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever.

Amen.
